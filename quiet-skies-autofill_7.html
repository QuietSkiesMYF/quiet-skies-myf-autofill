One-tap Favorite (2 min, one time)

Don't see a Share or Bookmarks icon in Safari? Go to Settings → Apps → Safari → Tabs, and switch the layout to "Bottom" or "Top" (not "Compact"). This keeps Share, Bookmarks, and Tabs always visible.

1. Open the city complaint form in Safari.
2. Tap the Share icon → Add Bookmark. Name it "Quiet Skies Fill."
3. Go to Bookmarks, tap Edit next to your new bookmark.
4. Replace the whole web address with the code below (tap Copy fill code here any time — it always grabs whatever's currently entered on the Complaint Form tab).
5. Save. Now, any time you're on the complaint form or on the page that appears right after you submit, tap that bookmark. On the complaint form, it fills everything in. On the "complaint submitted" page, it takes you straight to a fresh blank form — tap the bookmark once more there and that fresh form fills in too. Date and time are captured fresh every time you tap it.
